Balendra Shah (Independent) has secured 8,248 votes in Kathmandu Metropolitan City, leading Keshav Sthapit (CPN-UML) who has secured 4,342 votes in Nepal local elections 2022 (Sthaniya Chunab 2079). According to the Election Commission, Nepal, Kathmandu Metropolitan City has 300,242 eligible voters.

Kathmandu Metropolitan is in Kathmandu district of Bagmati Pradesh. There are 300,242 eligible voters for the Nepal local elections 2022, according to the Election Commission. There are 32 wards in the metropolitan city with the population of 845,767.
